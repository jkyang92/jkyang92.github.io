Research

My recent research focuses on multigraded syzygies, in particular virtual resolutions. But I also have a strong interest in syzygies more broadly as well as connections between Algebraic Geometry, Commutative Algebra, and Combinatorics.

My research has blended combinatorial, computation, algebraic techniques to study syzygies. This includes a particular focus on monomial ideals as well as toric varieties.
