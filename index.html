About Me

I am a Research Assistant Professor at Vanderbilt University, working Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry.

My recent research focuses on multigraded syzygies, in particular virtual resolutions. But I also have a strong interest in syzygies more broadly as well as connections between Algebraic Geometry, Commutative Algebra, and Combinatorics. For my papers and preprints, see the research tab.
